About Tesla Auto Charge

Tesla Auto Charge (also known as TAC) is a stand-alone Python-based application for controlling the charging of your personal Tesla car, based on elspot prices delivered by Nord Pool. The goal of the application is to lower the user's power bill by charge the vehicle while the electricity prices are low and stop charging when the prices are high.

The application is open-source and free to use by anyone.
